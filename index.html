WELCOME! I'M FRITZ.

This is a work in progress.

This page is an experiment in web design and certainly not one in personal branding. If you know me professionally, I am placing a lot of trust in you. I plan to keep some of my projects on here as well as mess around with HTML and CSS to learn the basics.

I am a big believer in open source and free software and have gotten quite tired of the corporate web and social media harvesting all of my data. This is my attempt to reclaim a small piece of the internet back for myself.

Here's a brief description of what you'll find:

  • Projects: Raspberry Pi builds, 3D prints, etc.
  • Consumerism: When I wake up from my fugue state and have sated my thirst for trinkets I will review them here
  • Blog: Occasional questionable thoughts.
  • About: Why are you still reading?
